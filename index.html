Frequently Asked Questions

Can I trade financial assets directly through the app?
No, you cannot trade financial assets or cryptocurrency directly through the Technical Analysis AI app. The app is designed for educational and analytical purposes only. Any trading activities are conducted through external platforms via affiliate links, specifically SimpleFX. The app serves as an analytical and educational tool to help you make informed decisions.
Can the app be personalized beyond the default assets shown?
Yes, the app provides analysis and insights for multiple assets beyond just PayPal, Microsoft, and General Electric. The app content is automatically updated with different trading opportunities across various financial instruments. Users can access short-term, mid-term, and long-term analysis across multiple market sectors and asset classes.
What types of analysis does the app provide?
The app provides AI-powered technical analysis including trend analysis, support and resistance levels, momentum indicators, and trading signals. Analysis is available across multiple timeframes (short-term, mid-term, and long-term) to accommodate different trading styles and investment approaches.
How accurate are the AI predictions?
While our AI algorithms are sophisticated and based on advanced market analysis techniques, no financial prediction can guarantee future results. The app provides educational analysis and insights to help inform your decision-making process. Always conduct your own research and consider consulting with financial professionals before making investment decisions.
Is my personal data secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. We follow industry-standard security practices and comply with Apple's privacy requirements. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.
How do I report a bug or technical issue?
If you encounter any bugs or technical issues, please contact us at tech@technicalanalysis-ai.com with a detailed description of the problem, your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce the issue. We typically respond within 12-24 hours.
Can I use the app on iPad?
Yes, the app is designed to work seamlessly on both iPhone and iPad with optimized interfaces for each device type. The iPad version takes advantage of the larger screen real estate to provide enhanced data visualization and improved user experience.
How often is the market data updated?
Market data and analysis are updated regularly to ensure you receive the most current information. The frequency of updates depends on market conditions and data availability from our sources.
